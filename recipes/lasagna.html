Delicious Lasagna

Description

This world famous lasagna is guranteed to be loved by all. It's flavorful and delicious!

Ingredients

  • Meats: ground beef and Italian sausage
  • Tomato Products: One can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, two cans of tomato paste
  • Seasonings: parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, black pepper
  • Cheeses: parmesan, mozarella, and ricotta cheese
  • One onion and two cloves of Garlic
  • Lasagna noodles
  • One Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasgna rest about 15 minutes before serving

How to Layer Lasagna

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan Cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan

How Long to Cook

The lasagna should take 50 minutes to cook in an oven preheated to 375 degrees F. Cover with foil for the first 25 minutes, then let it cook uncovered for the final 25 minutes. It is also important to let the lasagna rest at room temperature for about 15 minutes before you cut into it.
